        About the Missouri Assessment Program:
        
            Within the Office of College and Career Readiness, the Assessment section provides professional services related to the Missouri Assessment Program (MAP) and the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP).

The Assessment Section manages test development, on-going test maintenance, and oversees the test administration for four statewide, large-scale assessments. The MAP assessments test students progress toward mastery of the Missouri Show-Me Standards.

Grade-Level Assessments are augmented norm-referenced tests that are delivered annually each spring in communication arts and mathematics for grades 3-8, and science for grades 5 and 8.

        About Student-Teacher Ratio
        
Student/teacher ratio is calculated by dividing the total number of students by the total number of full-time equivalent teachers.  Please note that a smaller student/teacher ratio does not necessarily translate to smaller class size.  In some instances, schools hire teachers part time, and some teachers are hired for specialized instruction with very small class sizes. These and other factors contribute to the student/teacher ratio.  Note: For private schools, Student/teacher ratio may not include Pre-Kindergarten.
